Nemukai is a technology studio. We research, design, and engineer products that carry the wonder of the natural world — the richness of a coral reef, the warmth of a coastal sunset, the quiet surprise of something you've never seen before.

This isn't minimalism. A reef isn't simple. It's layered, alive, full of things worth noticing. That's the standard.

Home — [**nemukai.com**](https://nemukai.com)

### `> principles`

```
  01  wonder over polish         — reward a closer look
  02  craft is non-negotiable    — no ugly MVPs
  03  character over convention  — if it could belong to
                                   any studio, it doesn't
                                   belong to this one
  04  depth over breadth         — a pool is wide and
                                   shallow. a reef is
                                   focused and infinite.
  05  build the floor, not       — the things nobody sees,
      just the room                built with the same
                                   ambition as the things
                                   everybody does
```
